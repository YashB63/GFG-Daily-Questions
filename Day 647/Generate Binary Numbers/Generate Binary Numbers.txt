Given a number n. 

The task is to generate all binary numbers 
with decimal values from 1 to n.


Examples:

Input: n = 4

Output: ["1", "10", "11", "100"]

Explanation: Binary numbers from 
1 to 4 are 1, 10, 11 and 100.


Input: n = 6

Output: ["1", "10", "11", "100", "101", "110"]

Explanation: Binary numbers from 1 to 
6 are 1, 10, 11, 100, 101 and 110.


Constraints:
1 ≤ n ≤ 10^6
