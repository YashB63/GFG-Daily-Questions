Given a string s containing alphanumeric characters. 

You have to calculate the sum of all the numbers 
present in the string.


Examples:

Input: s = "1abc23"

Output: 24

Explanation: 1 and 23 are numbers in the 
string which is added to get the sum as 24.


Input: s = "geeks4geeks"

Output: 4

Explanation: 4 is the only number, 
so the sum is 4.


Constraints:

1 ≤ |s|≤ 10^5
The sum of Numbers ≤ 10^5
