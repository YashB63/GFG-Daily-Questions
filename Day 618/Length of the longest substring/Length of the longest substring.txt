Given a string s, find the length of the 
longest substring without repeating 
characters.


Examples:

Input: s = "geeksforgeeks"

Output: 7

Explanation: Longest substring is "eksforg".


Input: s = "abdefgabef"

Output: 6

Explanation: Longest substring are 
"abdefg" , "bdefga" and "defgab".


Input: s = "aaaaa"

Output: 1

Explanation: Longest substring is "a".


Constraints:

1 ≤ s.size() ≤ 10^5

It is guaranteed that all characters 
of the String s will be lowercase 
letters from 'a' to 'z'
