Given two integers d and n. 

Where d is the day, out of 7 days of the 
week, d varies from 0 to 6 as shown below.

0 - Sunday
1 - Monday
2 - Tuesday
3 - Wednesday
4 - Thursday
5 - Friday
6 - Saturday

You have to return the index for the day 
which is n days before the given day d.


Examples:

Input: d = 4, n = 3

Output: 1

Explanation: 3 days before the 4th is 1.


Input: d = 2, n = 19

Output: 4

Explanation: 19 days before the 2nd is 4.
