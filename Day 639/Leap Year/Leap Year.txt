You are given an Integer n. 

Return true if It is a Leap 
Year otherwise return false.


Examples:

Input: n = 4

Output: true

Explanation: 4 is not divisible by 100 
and is divisible by 4 so its a leap year


Input: n = 2021

Output: false

Explanation: 2021 is not divisible by 
100 and is also not divisible by 4 so 
its not a leap year


Constraints:
1<= n < 10^4
