Given an integer year. Print "True" 
(without quotes) if it can represent 
a leap year, otherwise print "False" 
(without quotes).


Examples:

Input: year = 2020

Output: True

Explanation: 2020 is leap year as it 
multilpe of 4 but not a multiple of 100.


Input: year = 2022

Output: False

Explanation: 2022 is not a leap year, 
as its neither multiple of 400 nor of 4.


Expected Complexities

Time Complexity: O(1)
Auxiliary Space: O(1)
