Given an integer i. 

Print the maximum number of nodes on level i 
of a binary tree.

Note: The level of a binary tree starts with 1 
(i.e., level 1 contains the root of the tree, 
level 2 contains the children of the root, and 
so on).


Examples:

Input: 5

Output: 16

Explanation: The maximum number of nodes at level 5 is 16.


Input: 1

Output: 1

Explanation: The maximum number of nodes at level 1 is 1.


Constraints:
1<= i <=20
