Given a positive integer n, you need to 
count the numbers less than or equal to 
n having exactly 9 divisors.


Examples :

Input: n = 100

Output: 2

Explanation: Numbers which have exactly 
9 divisors are 36 and 100.


Input: n = 200

Output: 3

Explanation: Numbers which have exactly 
9 divisors are 36, 100, 196. 


Constraints:
1 ≤ n ≤ 10^9
