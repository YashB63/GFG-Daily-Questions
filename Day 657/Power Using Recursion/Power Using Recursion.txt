You are given two numbers n and p. 

You need to find n^p.


Examples:

Input: n = 9, p = 9 

Output: 387420489

Explanation: 387420489 is the value obtained 
when 9 is raised to the power of 9.


Input: n = 2, p = 9

Output: 512

Explanation: 512 is the value obtained 
when 2 is raised to the power of 9.  


Constraints:

1 ≤ n ≤ 10
0 ≤  p ≤ 9
