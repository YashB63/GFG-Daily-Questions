You are given a string s consisting of multiple words. 

You need to count the total words in the string. 

Words are separated by a single space.

Note: It is guaranteed that the last character 
of the given string is not a white space.


Examples:

Input: s = "Geeks"

Output: 1

Explanation: There is just one word in the given sentence


Input: s = "World is hello"

Output: 3

Explanation: There exists three words in the given sentence


Constraints:
1 <= |s| <= 10^4
