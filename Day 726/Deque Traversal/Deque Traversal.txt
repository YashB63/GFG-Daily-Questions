Given a deque dq containing integer elements, 
the task is to traverse the dq and print its 
elements of it. 

Note: Print a newline at the end.


Examples:

Input: dq = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]

Output: 1 2 3 4 5

Explanation: dq will look like [1, 2, 3, 4, 5].


Input: dq = [1]

Output: 1

Explanation: dq will look like [1].


Constraints:
1 ≤ dq.size() ≤ 10^5
