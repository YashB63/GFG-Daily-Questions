Given a string s. 

Your task is to remove spaces from it. 


Examples:

Input: s = "geeks  for geeks"

Output: "geeksforgeeks"

Explanation: All the spaces have been removed.


Input: s = "    g f g"

Output: "gfg"

Explanation: All the spaces including 
the leading ones have been removed.


Constraints:
1<=|s|<=10^5
