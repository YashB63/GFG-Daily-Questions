Given 3 numbers a, b and c. 

Find the greatest number among them.


Examples:

Input: a = 10, b = 3, c = 2

Output: 10

Explanation: 10 is greatest among the three 


Input: a = -4, b = -3, c = -2

Output: -2

Explanation: -2 is greatest among the three


Constraints:
-10^9 ≤ a, b, c ≤ 10^9
